WELCOME TO OUR WORKFORCE



Simplifying Onboarding

Document Management



Managing the onboarding process and ensuring compliance with document collection can be a complex and time-consuming task. With Tresume's Workforce module, we simplify onboarding and document management by automating processes and providing a centralized platform for collecting and managing onboarding documents.




Key Features And Benefits



Document Collection



Streamline the collection of onboarding documents for different positions within your organization. Our platform allows you to create customized checklists, ensuring tha all necessary documents are collected from candidates, such as resumes, identification proofs, certifications, and more





Automated Reminders

Notifications



Never miss an important document again. Tresume's Workforce module sends automated reminders and notifications to candidates and HR teams, ensuring a timely collection of required documents and reducing manual follow-ups.




Secure Document Storage



Safely store and organize all onboarding documents in a secure and centralized location.Our platform ensures data privacy and compliance with industry regulations, providing peace of mind for HR teams and candidates.




Task Tracking and Completion



Keep track of the onboarding progress for each candidate with our intuitive task tracking system. HR teams can easily monitor the status of document collection, track completion rates, and identify any outstanding items.


Customizable Workflows



Tailor the onboarding process to match your organization's unique requirements. Tresume's Workforce module allows you to create customizable workflows, defining the specific steps and document requirements for each position, ensuring a standardized onboarding experience. Tresume's Workforce module simplifies onboarding and document management, reducing administrative burden, ensuring compliance, and providing a smooth onboarding experience for both candidates and HR teams.
